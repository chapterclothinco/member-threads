About Us

We Believe What You Wear Together Matters

Member Threads is the go-to for communities and organizations who want custom apparel that brings people together.

Member Threads is part of the Stitched family — the same manufacturing capabilities, the same team, the same commitment to quality. But with a specific focus: creating custom apparel for groups and communities who deserve better than generic merchandise.

We started Member Threads because we kept hearing the same story from nonprofits, clubs, and organizations: they wanted gear their members would actually wear, not just another logo slapped on a cheap tee. They wanted something that represented their community, their mission, their identity.

So that's what we do. We manufacture custom apparel from scratch — designing original artwork, selecting premium materials, and producing gear that looks and feels like it came from a real brand. Because it did. Yours.

Our Approach

We Know Groups

Committees. Approvals. Tight timelines. Budget constraints. We understand the reality of organizing for a group, and we've built our process to make it easy for organizers — not designers.

Quality Over Everything

Your gear represents your organization. That's why we use premium materials and professional-grade production. Gear your members will be proud to wear — at events and every day after.

Community First

We're not just vendors; we're partners in building community. We celebrate what brings people together, and we take pride in helping groups look and feel united.

What We Value

Community First

Everything we do is in service of bringing people together. We believe in the power of community, and we're honored to play a small part in helping groups unite around shared identity.

Quality That Lasts

We don't cut corners. Premium materials, professional production, quality control on every piece. Your gear should last — not just through the event, but for years of wear.

Making It Easy

We know you're juggling a lot. Our process is designed to take the apparel piece off your plate. Design help included. Clear communication. Reliable delivery.

Celebrating Belonging

There's something special about wearing what you're part of. We celebrate the communities, causes, and connections that bring people together.
